Semicircle is 90°: The angle subtended by a diameter at the circumference is a right angle.
Level 2 • Medium

Calculate the Angle

In the circle, if angle ABC = 30°, what is angle AOC?

[Diagram: Angle ABC at circumference and AOC at centre]
Explanation
The angle at the centre (AOC) is twice the angle at the circumference (ABC) when both are subtended by the same arc. Therefore, 2 × 30° = 60°.
